ADA compliance FAQ

Is my Shopify store ADA compliant?
Not automatically. Shopify is only the platform. Your live theme, apps, product media, custom components, navigation, forms, and checkout experience still need to meet WCAG requirements.
How much does an ADA compliance lawsuit cost?
The full cost usually includes legal fees, settlement pressure, emergency remediation, consultant time, and internal distraction. For most brands, proactive WCAG fixes cost less than reacting after a demand letter.
What is WCAG 2.2?
WCAG 2.2 is the newest accessibility guideline update covering areas like focus visibility, drag-and-drop interactions, target size, and authentication flows. It is the practical standard many teams use to evaluate storefront accessibility now.
